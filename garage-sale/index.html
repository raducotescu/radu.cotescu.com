Monitors - Dell P2715Q - CHF 230 each, CHF 420 together

I have two Dell P2715Q 4K monitors for sale. They're in perfect functioning order and come with all of their original cables and transport boxes. They are the A05 hardware revision, so they do support HDMI 2.0 and it's enabled out of the box.

Dell doesn't list the product any more, but B&H still sells them in the US and has a good specs page.

They have a great IPS panel, a very crisp image, extremely good colour reproduction (they are factory calibrated to 99% sRGB) and overall have a good set of ports, albeit not the most modern ones any more. The stands with which they come allow tilt, pivot, swivel and height adjustments.
